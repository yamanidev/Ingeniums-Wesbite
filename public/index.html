What is INGENIUMS?

Ingeniums is a cultural and scientific club based in the higher school of computer science in Sidi-Bel-Abbes, a place where the members and the other students exchange their knowledge and learn from each other.

Join us!

Who are we?

We are a group of passionate students interested in everything related to computer science, we collaborate to make events that help us and the others to develop ourselves and build future careers, our values are: Connect, Learn, Share, Innovate, and Create. And we also share the following visions: spreading knowledge, helping the community, and have fun!
